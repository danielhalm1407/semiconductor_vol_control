The Link: Semi Conductor Vol Control

A study on methodologies to reduce downside risk and negative skew with exposure to semiconductor equities.

Step 1

Start with an underlying semiconductor etf: the iShares Semiconductor ETF (SOXX)

Step 2

Backtest a handful of simple vol control measures implemented with daily rebalancing based on the ETF

Step 3

Observe the very 'best' volatility control theoretically possible:

  1. Go into cash if index went down at rebalancing

  2. Go into 10-year treasuries if the index went down at rebalancing

  3. Go into mixed, uncorrelated assets if the index went down at rebalancing

  4. Go short on the index if the index went down at rebalancing

Step 4

Attempt to identify the most appropriate indicative signals of an approaching index or sector downturn:

  1. MACD crosses below 4 period exponential moving average (EMA) of MACD.
